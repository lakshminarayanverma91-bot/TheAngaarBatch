Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! All mock interview sessions are 100% free. We believe in trust and want to help students succeed.

How long is each session?

Each mock interview session is 60 minutes long, including time for questions and feedback.

Can I reschedule my interview?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before your scheduled session by contacting us.

What platform do you use?

We conduct all sessions via Zoom. You'll receive a meeting link 24 hours before your interview.

How many sessions can I book?

You can book multiple sessions, but we recommend waiting at least 1 week between sessions to implement feedback.
